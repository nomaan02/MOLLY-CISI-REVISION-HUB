# CISI Level 6 Regulation & Compliance — Study Hub

**Exam date: 12 June 2025**

This is your AI-powered revision environment for the CISI Diploma in Investment Compliance — Level 6 Regulation & Compliance exam. Everything you need is pre-loaded and organised. The AI knows the textbook, the past papers, and the exam structure.

## How to Use It

1. **Open this project in Cursor**
2. **Open Agent chat** (the chat panel on the right)
3. **Ask questions** — the AI acts as your private compliance tutor

That's it. No setup needed.

Here are some things you can ask:

- **"Explain the SM&CR certification regime"** — get a clear, exam-focused explanation
- **"What are the six general principles of the Takeover Code?"** — quick factual recall
- **"Quiz me on financial promotions"** — get practice questions from real past papers
- **"Give me a Section B practice question on market abuse"** — get a scenario with sub-questions
- **"Help me plan a Section C essay on ESG regulation"** — get an essay structure with key points
- **"What topics should I focus on this week?"** — get a prioritised study plan
- **"What's the IRAC structure for answering Section B?"** — get exam technique coaching
- **"Explain POCA s327-334 for the exam"** — get the detail you need, nothing more

## Tips

- **Ask for practice questions** after studying a topic — the AI has 333 real past paper questions with model answers
- **Request exam-style answers** — the AI will coach you to write in the format the examiner expects (headings not bullet points, IRAC structure for scenarios, balanced essays)
- **Ask "what does the examiner look for?"** — the AI has all the examiner quotes and marking guidance
- **Don't waste time on Chapters 4 and 5** — they're only 2.5% of the exam each. The AI will tell you this too.
- **Focus on Chapter 2** (40% of the exam) and **Chapter 6** (15%) — that's over half the marks
