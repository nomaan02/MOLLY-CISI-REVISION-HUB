# Knowledge Base Index

## Priority Study Order

Based on lecturer's chapter exam weights (not textbook chapter sequence):

| Priority | Chapter | Exam Weight | Files | What to Focus On |
|----------|---------|-------------|-------|-----------------|
| 1 | Chapter 2 — FCA Handbook & PRA Rulebook | 40% | 17 topic files | SM&CR, PRIN, Consumer Duty, SYSC, COBS (all D-level). This is the main event. |
| 2 | Chapter 6 — Risk in Financial Services | 15% | 2 topic files | CISI Code of Conduct, BIS 10 compliance principles. High ROI — short content, big marks. Underpins the compulsory B question. |
| 3 | Chapter 1 — Regulatory Framework | 20% | 4 topic files | FSMA 2000 (Parts I-VIII), regulated activities, authorisation. Dominates Section A. |
| 4 | Chapter 3 — Other Regulatory Provisions | 20% | 12 topic files | Market abuse, insider dealing, money laundering, bribery, takeover code. All D-level. |
| 5 | Chapter 5 — Current Developments | 2.5% | 1 file | Skim for Section C essay background. Don't memorise. |
| 6 | Chapter 4 — Markets and Exchanges | 2.5% | 1 file | Know the definitions (RIE, MTF, OTF, SI) and move on. |

**95% of the exam is Chapters 1, 2, 3, and 6.**

6. Market abuse / insider dealing — MAR, CJA 1993, defences (Ch3, 20%)
7. Financial promotions — s21, COBS 4, social media (Ch2, 40%)
8. Client categorisation and suitability — COBS 3, 9/9A (Ch2, 40%)
9. Money laundering / POCA / MLR — three stages, SARs, JMLSG (Ch3, 20%)
